Privacy Policy for Videre: Photo & Video Viewer

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Videre: Photo & Video Viewer is a photo and video viewer for macOS developed by Christoph Weidinger.

Videre: Photo & Video Viewer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

• The app has no user accounts and requires no sign-in.
• The app does not contain any analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies.
• Videre: Photo & Video Viewer only accesses the files and folders you explicitly open. It runs fully sandboxed by macOS, and your files never leave your device.
• App settings (such as your chosen default formats and image rotations) are stored locally on your Mac using the system's standard preferences and never leave your device.
• Purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. Videre: Photo & Video Viewer never sees or stores your payment information. Any data related to your purchase is processed by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy.

Contact: If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact christoph.weidinger.work@gmail.com.
