Privacy Policy for Videre

Last updated: 8 July 2026

Videre is a photo and video viewer for macOS developed by Christoph Weidinger.

Videre does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

• The app has no user accounts and requires no sign-in.
• The app does not contain any analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies.
• Videre only accesses the files and folders you explicitly open. It runs fully sandboxed by macOS, and your files never leave your device.
• App settings (such as your chosen default formats and image rotations) are stored locally on your Mac using the system's standard preferences and never leave your device.
• Purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. Videre never sees or stores your payment information. Any data related to your purchase is processed by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy.

Contact: If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact christoph.weidinger.work@gmail.com.
